home // Assessment & Curriculum

Onelern

At Pre-Primary level, we follow Cambridge curriculum which suffice all the child’s intellectual and physical needs and prepare them to receive the future education that will be imparted to them in right way. We enrich the learning through fine motor and gross motor skills. Through this students develop an informed curiosity and a lasting passion for learning.

At primary level (G I-V), we follow onelern curriculum an one stop digital solution partner to empower educators, learners and management. It is a learner centered Holistic development program. It aims in developing:

  • Emotional, Social, Ethical learning
  • Cognitive thinking
  • Physical, Motor and Mental Wellbeing
  • Creative and Aesthetic skills
  • Technology

Based on the norms of NEP 2020 and imparting CBSE Board. We provide Onelern with connected classrooms, Grades, rewards, leader boards, online and offline learning. At Middle level and senior level, we follow NCERT curriculum which not promote rote learning but provides a competitive edge at the national level after schooling. Students have flexibility in choosing their subject skills and capacities. We have also added a skill subject along with the main subjects the skill subject develops digital literacy, coding, computational thinking, ethical and moral reasoning.

Assessment for Grade I and II are done in a regular pictographic and animatic method. From G III-X assessment is done based on Pen and Paper test and Terminal tests as per the norms of CBSE. Assessments are done by competency-based problems to assess the learning outcomes on the competencies. We follow SAFAL (Structured Assessment for Analyzing learning) prescribed by CBSE. SAFAL provides diagnostic date and insights to each student’s core competencies.
